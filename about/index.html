Who is R.E.C.L.U.S.E.?

R.E.C.L.U.S.E. is the alias of Marley Turnbull, young hip-hop producer and record collector from Byron Bay, Australia.

The project began in December 2024.

R.E.C.L.U.S.E. is currently laying the foundation to support what will become a prolific catalog of sample-based hip-hop pieces.

The best place to support is through Bandcamp, at this early stage, where core listeners get early access to new music before it hits other streaming services.
